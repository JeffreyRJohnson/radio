I am the owner of Route 66 Radio Communications. I have been in the 2-way radio business for more that 40 years. My company is a authorized Motorola dealer. Our business is sales, service and rentals of 2-way radios. We have a "wide area" coverage UHF trunking system for radio dispatch located on Sandia Crest. You can always reach me, 24/7, by calling (505) 440-6257
